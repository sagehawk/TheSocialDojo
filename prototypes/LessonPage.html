The Art of the Follow-Up Question

The Mental Model

Don't be an Interviewer, be an Explorer.

Interviewers ask "what." Explorers ask "how" and "why."

The Common Pitfall

Most conversations die when we treat them like a checklist. When you get an answer, don't just move on to the next topic.
